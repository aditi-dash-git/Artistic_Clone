Shoe Size Certification

Finding the true size of the shoe is hard. The shoe is usually longer and bigger than the foot it was made to fit. True size depends on multiple dimensions & shapes – Heel to Toe length, Toebox shape, shoe width, and toe height to name a few. It also depends on design factors such as fastening (laces or trim), counter design, padding, material. Findmeashoe uses precision 3D technology to measure the shoe geometry and uses Artificial Intelligence to do the fit assessment. In it is scanning facility, the company is certifying 100+ pairs every day. Size Certificates offered are digital. Findmeashoe offers an API that can be used to query and display the True size and Fit Tip for the certified shoes.

"Get Me" Endless Aisle Solution for In-Stores

Using our flagship mobile camera-based scanning technology, we have launched the 2.5D foot scanner for Footwear stores across the globe. The scanner allows the end consumer to scan their feet in the store within 30 seconds and provides a SizeID which contains information regarding feet, well beyond foot length. Many stores still use metal scales that are 100 years old, which provide an indication of your size based on length. In order to provide personalized recommendations to make a purchase decision, there are very few tools other than relying on the sales force's knowledge and training. With our scanner and AI-enabled SizeID service, personalized fit recommendations can be provided to each customer who walks inside the store.
